Cricket Golf Carts are collapsible mini golf carts that are perfect for campers and RV enthusiasts that want to take a golf cart along for the ride, but don’t have the room for a regular sized one. The Cricket Mini Golf Cart is one of the most versatile golf carts in the world.

The Cricket RX-5 is basically the same as our popular SX-3 but adds updated styling, front suspension for better handling and ride, and a column mounted turn signals switch. The Cricket RX 5 Mobility Golf Cart is also 2″ taller than the SX-3 when folded up because of the added suspension. Highlights include:
  • Two-passenger rear seat
  • Folds down to 30 inches
  • 10-13 mph travel speed
  • 30-mile run-time
  • 36-volt system
