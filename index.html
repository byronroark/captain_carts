Cricket SX-3

For a little more speed, power, and passenger room, the Cricket SX-3 is a step up from the ESV and provides all of that. Based on the design of the original Cricket, the SX-3 is a little closer to a standard golf cart for those with more need. Highlights include:
  • Two-passenger rear seat
  • Folds down to 28 inches
  • 10-13 mph travel speed
  • 30-mile run-time
  • 36-volt system
  • 303 lbs
